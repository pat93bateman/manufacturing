High-Caliber Machining

Count on us to provide you with CNC machining services to facilitate your project seamlessly. We promise precision results and total customer satisfaction, no matter the scope or specs of your project. With a primary function of forming and shaping different materials, lathes have a wide range of industry applications.

Our broad-ranging capabilities and equipment distinguish the work we do. Our services ensure exact tolerance and precise positioning on large or small components with simple or complex shapes. Extensive experience machining iron castings, aluminum castings, and bronze castings to exacting tolerances.

Milling

Parts can be fabricated completely through milling or milling can be used as a secondary process to add or refine features on parts that were manufactured using a different process. Our high-speed milling is performed with precision and efficiency, producing the components you need quickly and to-spec.

Turning

Our turning capabilities include CNC lathes with both conventional and Swiss turning. We can turn a broad range of alloys and produce components for complex systems with tight tolerances. When companies need accurate parts no matter how small, Owens Manufacturing provides them with high-quality lathed and turned components that can't be found anywhere else.
